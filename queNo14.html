List of Movies

  • The Dark Knight

    Release Year: 2008

    Summary: Batman, aided by his allies and Commissioner Gordon, sets out to dismantle the remaining criminal organizations that plague Gotham City, but soon finds himself facing a new and ruthless enemy, the Joker.

  • Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge

    Release Year: 1995

    Summary: Raj and Simran meet on a trip to Europe and fall in love. However, Simran's father has already arranged her marriage with someone else. They must fight for their love and convince Simran's father to change his mind.

  • Super Deluxe

    Release Year: 2019

    Summary: Super Deluxe is a Tamil-language film that revolves around the lives of various interconnected characters, including a transgender woman, a couple dealing with infidelity, and a young boy who discovers a shocking secret.

  • Inception

    Release Year: 2010

    Summary: Inception follows a skilled thief who is capable of entering people's dreams and stealing their secrets. He is hired to perform the reverse - to plant an idea into someone's mind. As the mission unfolds, the line between dreams and reality becomes blurred.

  • Baahubali: The Beginning

    Release Year: 2015

    Summary: Set in the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati, the film follows the story of Shivudu, a young warrior who embarks on a journey to discover his true identity and save the kingdom from an evil king. The film is filled with epic battles and stunning visual effects.

  • Avengers: Endgame

    Release Year: 2019

    Summary: In the epic conclusion to the Avengers saga, the surviving members of the superhero team must come together one last time to undo the devastating effects of Thanos' snap and restore order to the universe.
