List of Famous Historical Battles

  • Battle of Thermopylae

    Date: 480 BCE

    The Battle of Thermopylae was fought between an alliance of Greek city-states, led by King Leonidas I of Sparta, and the Persian Empire. The Greeks held off the Persian forces for several days at the narrow pass of Thermopylae, but were eventually defeated.

  • Battle of Hastings

    Date: 1066

    The Battle of Hastings was fought between the Norman-French army of Duke William II of Normandy and the English army led by King Harold II. It resulted in the Norman conquest of England, marking a significant turning point in English history.

  • Battle of Waterloo

  • Battle of Gettysburg

    Date: 1863

    The Battle of Gettysburg was a significant battle fought during the American Civil War. It took place in and around the town of Gettysburg, Pennsylvania, between the Confederate Army of Northern Virginia, led by General Robert E. Lee, and the Union Army of the Potomac, led by General George G. Meade. The battle resulted in a Union victory and is often considered a turning point in the war.

  • Battle of Stalingrad

    Date: 1942-1943

    The Battle of Stalingrad was a major battle on the Eastern Front of World War II. It was fought between the Soviet Union and Nazi Germany. The battle was a turning point in the war, as the Soviet forces successfully defended the city of Stalingrad and inflicted heavy casualties on the German army, marking the beginning of the Soviet offensive that would eventually lead to the defeat of Nazi Germany.
