List of Landmarks

  • Eiffel Tower

    Location: Paris, France

    The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.

  • Taj Mahal

    Location: Agra, India

    The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located on the right bank of the Yamuna River in Agra. It is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.

  • Statue of Liberty

    Location: New York, United States

    The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. It is a symbol of freedom and democracy.

  • Great Wall of China

    Location: China

    The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials. It is the longest wall in the world, spanning over 13,000 miles.

  • Sydney Opera House

    Location: Sydney, Australia

    The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts center located in Sydney Harbor. It is renowned for its unique and distinctive architectural design.
